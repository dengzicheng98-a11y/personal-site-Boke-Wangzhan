Rule 01 — feature-wide
aspect > 1.95 → 100vw, standalone. Never paired or trio'd. Visual accent.
Rule 02 — feature-tall
aspect < 1.60 → centered min(75vw,1200px), standalone. Would dwarf its neighbours in a trio.
Rule 03 — triptych
only aspect 1.70 – 1.85 qualifies. Three frames, equal height, no exceptions.
Rule 04 — pair-port
diptych-vertical: deprecated. Real shoots don't pair like that. Removed from the system.
Rule 05 — long-tail break
horiz-streak ≥ 5 → next block forced to feature-wide. Counter resets on any vertical / quote.

On the rules

The layout system is aspect-aware: every block reads its photo's aspect ratio and routes it to the appropriate template. feature-wide and feature-tall are escape hatches — outliers don't get squeezed into a trio just to fill a row. The long-tail break keeps the eye from drowning in a string of similar frames.
